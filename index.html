About Super Muay Thai

About Super Muay Thai offers unparalleled Muay Thai instruction to men, women, and children across Hong Kong. Founded in 2012 by Thailand Champion has built a loyal following of fitness enthusiasts and martial arts devotees.

Super Muay Thai Gym

The Best Martial Arts School in the city, Super Muay Thai brings to you a modern and intense experience! Super is the only Martial Arts school can gives clients a fusion of both Fitness and Martial Arts, making our classes and Personal Training in Hong Kong.

Did you know?

Muay Thai is an ancient fighting art referred to as the "Art of Eight Limbs". It makes use of punches, kicks, elbows and knee strikes, learned and perfected several hundred years ago.
